Scavenger Hunt

BRANDING | ILLUSTRATION

Scavenger Hunt is a Facebook game made by an independent game studio called Luetso. Luetso was a previous client of mine that I designed a logo for, and decided to bring me on into the development of this game to create all of the visual assets that they needed. I chose a tabletop setting, using a weathered picnic table and including shadows from leaves above the table to create the impression of an outdoor, wooded setting.
